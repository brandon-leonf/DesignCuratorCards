# CURATOR NOTES

## Bauhaus
- Founded 1919 in Weimar by Walter Gropius
- Goal: unify art, craft, and industry
- Vorkurs = foundation course
- Focus on geometry and functionalism
- Closed in 1933 by Nazi regime

## Swiss Style
- 1950s Switzerland
- Grid systems and mathematical spacing
- Helvetica and sans-serif typography
- Objective communication
- Photography over illustration

## Grunge Typography
- 1990s reaction against Swiss order
- David Carson and Ray Gun magazine
- Distressed, layered, deconstructed type
- Legibility sometimes secondary

## Connections
Bauhaus → modernist foundation  
Swiss Style → extreme order and clarity  
Grunge → rebellion against order
